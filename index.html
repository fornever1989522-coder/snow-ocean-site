Snow Ocean Travel Consulting

Simple planning for snow, sea, and mountain trips.

What We Do

Snow Ocean is a small travel consulting agency that helps travelers plan straightforward, adventure-focused trips. We focus on three types of travel:

  • Snowboarding and ski vacations
  • Scuba diving trips and island stays
  • Hiking and trekking itineraries

How We Help

We don’t sell flights or hotel rooms directly. Instead, we help you decide where to go, when to go, and how to structure your days:

  • Choosing resorts, dive shops, and hiking routes
  • Rough day-by-day trip outlines
  • Basic budget and timing suggestions

About Snow Ocean

Snow Ocean Travel Consulting is a small, independent travel advisor based in Dallas, Texas. We focus on practical, experience-based advice for people who want to spend less time searching and more time enjoying the mountains, ocean, and trails.

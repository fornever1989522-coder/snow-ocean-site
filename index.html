About Snow Ocean

Snow Ocean Travel Consulting is a small, independent travel advisory based in Dallas, Texas. We focus on adventure travel: snowboarding and ski trips, scuba diving holidays, and hiking or trekking routes around the world.

Our role is to help you decide where to go, when to go, and how to structure your trip so you get the most out of your time on the mountain, in the water, or on the trail. We don’t sell flights or hotel packages directly; instead we provide experience-based guidance and simple trip outlines that you can book yourself through the platforms you prefer.

Services

Snowboarding & Ski Trips

Suggestions on destinations, seasons, and resort areas for Japan powder trips, Colorado and Utah, Canada, and Europe, plus high-level day-by-day pacing.

Scuba Diving Trips

Guidance for dive regions such as the Philippines, Indonesia, Maldives, and Thailand: where to base, how long to stay, and how to combine islands or dive spots in one trip.

Hiking & Trekking Plans

Itinerary ideas for multi-day treks including Patagonia, Kilimanjaro, Nepal (EBC), and national park routes, focusing on route choice, pacing, and basic logistics.
